## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
*   Click **"Register"** on the home screen.
*   Enter your email and create a secure password.
*   **Tip**: Complete your **Health Profile** right away! The more Florence knows about your allergies, conditions, and goals, the better her advice will be.

### 2. Meet Your Dashboard
Your central hub for health.
*   **Vitals Card**: See your BMI, weight trends, and blood pressure at a glance.
*   **Daily Goals**: Check off today's tasks (e.g., "Drink 2L water", "Walk 5000 steps").
*   **Upcoming Appointments**: Never miss a doctor's visit.

## ü§ñ Using Florence AI

Florence is powered by advanced AI to help you make better health decisions. She is not a doctor, but she is a very smart companion.

### What can I ask Florence?
*   **Diet**: "Plan a gluten-free breakfast for me."
*   **Fitness**: "I have 20 minutes. What's a good home workout?"
*   **Symptoms**: "I have a headache and dry eyes. What could it be?" *(Note: Florence provides information, not diagnosis)*.
*   **Wellness**: "Help me lower my stress levels."

**How to Chat**: Look for the **"Ask Florence"** button or the chat icon in the bottom right corner of your screen.

## üèÜ Earning Rewards (HAIC Tokens)

We believe healthy habits should be rewarded. That's why we created **Health AI Coins (HAIC)**.

### How to Earn
1.  **Hit Your Goals**: Complete daily health tasks like steps, water intake, or sleep targets.
2.  **Follow Dietary Plans**: Stick to the meal plans Florence creates for you.
3.  **Attend Appointments**: successfully completing visits with doctors.

### Your Wallet
*   Check your balance in the **"Rewards"** tab.
*   Your tokens are secured on the **XRP Ledger**, a secure digital ledger.
*   *Coming Soon: Use tokens for discounts on consultations and health products!*
